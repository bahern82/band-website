Two Monkeys On Tour

Two Monkees On: First Ever Michael Nesmith and Micky Dolenz Tour Starting in June New York— The Monkees’ Michael Nesmith and Micky Dolenz are hitting the road in June for their first-ever tour as a duo. They’re calling the tour The Monkees Present: The Mike and Micky Show (as opposed to simply The Monkees) because there are only two members touring.

New Micky Dolenz Album

New Micky Dolenz Live Album “Out Of Nowhere” Coming in April 2018 From 7A Records 7A Records presents a brand new album from Micky Dolenz – Out Of Nowhere. Recorded in April this year with the sublime 30 piece American Metropole Orchestra, Dolenz sings all of the Monkees’ greatest hits along with some outstanding deep album blues songs.
